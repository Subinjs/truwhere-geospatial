Surveying and Aerial Mapping

TruWhere specializes in comprehensive surveying and aerial mapping services that provide accurate and detailed spatial data for various industries and applications. Our experienced team utilizes advanced technologies and techniques to deliver high-quality surveying and mapping solutions.

Boundary Surveys

Our boundary surveys accurately determine property lines, helping property owners and developers establish legal boundaries for construction, land division, or property disputes. We employ precise surveying methods to ensure accurate and reliable results.

Topographical Surveys

TruWhere conducts topographical surveys to capture detailed information about the shape and characteristics of land surfaces. Our surveys provide essential data for infrastructure development, urban planning, and engineering projects, enabling effective design and analysis.

Construction Surveys

We offer construction surveys to support the planning and execution of construction projects. Our surveys provide accurate measurements, stakeout points, and as-built documentation, ensuring that construction activities align with design specifications.

Sub-centimeter GPS Surveying

TruWhere utilizes sub-centimeter GPS surveying techniques to achieve precise mapping and data collection. This level of accuracy is ideal for applications that require high precision, such as land surveying, asset management, and infrastructure planning.

GPS Mapping for Environmental and Natural Resource Management

We leverage GPS mapping technologies to support environmental and natural resource management. Our mapping solutions enable the collection of geospatial data related to vegetation, water resources, habitats, and other environmental factors, facilitating effective conservation and management strategies.

High-Resolution Aerial Photography

TruWhere offers high-resolution aerial photography services using state-of-the-art drones and aerial imaging technology. We capture detailed imagery from various angles and perspectives, providing valuable visual information for planning, analysis, and documentation purposes.

3D Modeling and Visualization

Our 3D modeling and visualization services allow clients to visualize landscapes, structures, and objects in a virtual environment. This technology facilitates enhanced spatial understanding, design visualization, and simulation for architectural, engineering, and marketing purposes.

Digital Elevation Models (DEMs) and Terrain Analysis

We generate digital elevation models (DEMs) and perform terrain analysis to assess landforms, slopes, and elevation variations. This information is crucial for flood modeling, viewshed analysis, slope stability assessment, and other geospatial applications.

TruWhere is dedicated to delivering reliable and accurate surveying and aerial mapping services. Contact us today to discuss your specific project requirements and leverage our expertise for your spatial data needs.
